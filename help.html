🚀 Getting Started

Sidedoor is a private contact-sharing app for professionals and their clients. Professionals set a secret word. They tell it to clients in person. Clients download Sidedoor, type the word, and instantly get the professional's direct contact — no booking platform, no commission, no middleman.
Search "Sidedoor" on the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android). Download and install it like any other app. It's free to download for both professionals and clients.
Professionals need an account — this is where you set your secret word and manage your subscription. Clients in most cases just need to enter the word; a lightweight account may be created automatically to track your connections.
Not yet — Sidedoor is currently a mobile app only. A web companion is planned for a future update.

Words are exact-match and case-sensitive. Check: (1) correct spelling, (2) no extra spaces, (3) capitals match what you were told. Still not working? Ask your professional to confirm their word — they may have reset it.

💳 Billing & Subscription

Professionals pay £1/month. That's it. Clients are always free. No setup fee, no annual lock-in.
Go to Profile → Subscription → Cancel. You can cancel any time — no questions, no retention flow. Your word stays active until the end of the billing period, then deactivates.
Your secret word deactivates. New clients won't be able to connect with it. Clients who already connected still have your contact — that doesn't disappear. You can reactivate any time and your word becomes live again.
We handle refunds case by case. Email hello@sidedoor.app and we'll sort it out.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Try: (1) force-close and reopen, (2) restart your phone, (3) delete and reinstall Sidedoor. If it still won't open, email us at hello@sidedoor.app with your device model and OS version.
Check you have a network connection. Make sure the word is exact (case-sensitive, no spaces). If it says "word not found," the professional may have reset their word — ask them for the new one.
Sidedoor needs permission to access your contacts to save Steve Work. Go to your phone Settings → Sidedoor → Contacts → Allow. Then re-enter the word in the app.
Use the "Forgot?" link on the login screen to reset your access. If you signed up with a phone number, you'll get a verification code. Still locked out? Email hello@sidedoor.app.
