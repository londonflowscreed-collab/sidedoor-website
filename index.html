FAQ

Questions answered.

Is my employer really unable to see this?

Yes. Sidedoor doesn't post anything publicly, doesn't connect to LinkedIn, and doesn't appear anywhere that would identify you as running a side business. The app is disguised in your contacts under a name you choose, and can wear a generic icon. There's no social profile, no public listing, and no way for anyone to discover you're using it unless you tell them your word.

What happens if I change my secret word?

Instantly, the old word stops working for everyone. Anyone who tries the previous word will get nothing — your number is completely shielded. You simply tell your current clients your new word when you see them next. This is especially useful if you move to a new employer or want a clean slate.

Can a client share the word with someone I don't know?

Technically yes — a word can be passed on. But you're in control: if you find unexpected contacts coming through, you can change your word at any time. Think of it like a password — share it wisely, change it when needed. Most users treat it as an informal referral system.

Do clients need to create an account?

Yes — clients sign up with an email. It's £1/month with a 3-month minimum commitment. The account keeps your lookup history private and lets you message professionals directly in the app.

Who is Sidedoor for?

Anyone who builds personal relationships with clients in their job — and wants to maintain those relationships privately. Hairdressers, personal trainers, physios, massage therapists, tutors, coaches, interior designers, tradespeople, financial advisors, and anyone else who works with people directly. If you'd rather your clients contacted you personally rather than going back through an employer or platform, Sidedoor is for you.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Through the App Store or Google Play, exactly like any other subscription. No hidden retention flows, no "are you sure?" obstacles. Cancel in two taps. If you rejoin later, you'll get a new word and start fresh.
