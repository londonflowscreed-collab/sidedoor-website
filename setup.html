Setup Guide

Get started in 5 minutes

Whether you're a professional setting your secret word, or a client connecting to someone you trust — this guide walks you through it.

Step by step

For Professionals

You're the one setting the word. You're the one who decides who gets it. Here's how to get up and running.

1

Download Sidedoor

Search for Sidedoor on the App Store or Google Play, or tap a download link from this site. Install it like any other app.

2

Create your account

Sign up with your phone number or email. This is your professional account — it links to your contact info so clients can reach you directly.

💡 Use the number or email your clients will actually message you on. Whatever you add here is what they'll receive when they connect.
3

Set your secret word

Tap Set my word in the app. Choose something memorable but not too obvious — this is the key you'll whisper to clients you trust.

Good examples: mango   friday   tiger22

Avoid: your name, "password", anything a stranger could guess.

🔒 You can change your word at any time. Clients who already connected keep your contact — only new connections need the updated word.
4

Tell clients — in person, quietly

Next time you see a client you trust: say "Download Sidedoor, type in your word — it goes straight to my private number." That's it.

No texts with the word in. No writing it down. Just say it.

🗣️ The magic of Sidedoor is that there's no digital trail. You said a word. They typed it. No record anywhere.
5

You're done

As clients connect, they'll appear in your connected list inside the app. You can see how many people have your word — and reset it any time if you need to start fresh.

  • Your secret word is active 24/7
  • Clients get your direct contact immediately
  • No notifications, no drama — it just works
  • £1/month. Cancel any time from the app.

Step by step

For Clients

Your professional gave you a word. Here's how to use it to get their direct contact — takes about two minutes.

1

Download Sidedoor — it's free

Search for Sidedoor on the App Store or Google Play. Download and install it. Sidedoor is completely free for clients — no subscription, no fees, ever.

2

Open the app and enter the word

When you open Sidedoor, you'll see a simple box. Type in the secret word your professional gave you — exactly as they said it.

⚠️ Words are case-sensitive. If it's not working, check capitals and try again. Still stuck? Ask your professional to confirm the word.
3

Get their direct contact instantly

The moment the word matches, you'll receive your professional's direct contact details. Tap to save them to your phone.

That's it. You now have their number — no platform, no booking system, no middleman.

4

It saves as "Steve Work"

Sidedoor doesn't sit in your app drawer with a logo. Instead, it appears in your contacts as a person called Steve Work. Tap that contact and the app opens.

Nobody knows it's an app. Nobody asks who Steve Work is. That's the point.

📱 Don't delete Steve Work from your contacts — that's how you access Sidedoor. If you delete it, just reinstall the app and re-enter the word.
5

Follow them anywhere

If your professional moves salon, gym, or company — their Sidedoor contact moves with them. Their number stays the same. You stay connected.

  • Free for clients — always
  • No account needed in most cases
  • No public profile — you're invisible too
  • Works even if they change jobs or locations

Common questions

Quick answers

The word isn't working

Check for capitals and spaces. Words are exact-match. If still broken, ask your professional — they may have reset their word.

I deleted Steve Work by accident

Re-download Sidedoor and enter the word again. You'll get the contact back instantly.

Can my employer see I've used this?

No. Sidedoor has no public profiles, no searchable directory, and creates no traceable record. The only thing that happened was: someone said a word, and you typed it.

Is Sidedoor free?

For clients — yes, always free. Professionals pay £1/month.
