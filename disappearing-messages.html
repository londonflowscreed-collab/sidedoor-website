The difference

Not Signal. Not WhatsApp. Something different.

Signal and WhatsApp are about securing messages in transit. Sidedoor is about messages leaving no visible trace on your phone.

Signal / WhatsApp

Encrypted in transit

Your messages are protected while they travel. But once they arrive, they sit on both phones — in the conversation thread, in backups, in notification history. Anyone who picks up either phone can read them. Signal has an optional disappearing messages feature, but it's not the core experience.

Sidedoor

Disappearing by default

Every message in Sidedoor has a timer. When it expires, the message is gone from both devices — no archive, no recoverable thread. It's not an optional setting or a feature you need to remember to switch on. It's simply how the app works. Discretion is the default.
